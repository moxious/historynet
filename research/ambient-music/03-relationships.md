## Relationship Type Legend

| Type | Directed | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| `influenced` | Yes | Source directly influenced target's work/thinking |
| `collaborated_with` | No | Worked together on recordings, performances, or projects |
| `taught` | Yes | Formal teacher-student relationship |
| `studied_under` | Yes | Source studied under target |
| `produced` | Yes | Source produced recordings for target (custom type) |

### 1. Leon Theremin → Clara Rockmore
- **Type**: `taught`

- **Evidence**: Rockmore studied with Theremin after meeting him in New York; they collaborated on improving the instrument's range and responsiveness.

- **Strength**: strong
